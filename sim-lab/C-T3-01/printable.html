BRANCH (improper): The crew either failed to place pads pre-emptively or, after arrest, defaulted to a CPR cycle 'per algorithm' before defibrillating despite the witnessed-with-pads opportunity. First shock is delayed by 90-180 seconds. During this time the coarse VFib deteriorates toward fine VFib — a less perfusable rhythm with markedly lower defibrillation success. The teaching beat: witnessed arrest with pads is a SHOCK-FIRST scenario. Inserting a CPR cycle is a judgment error specific to witnessed-with-pads events.
