Go/No-Go Airway Decision
Stridor worsening or SpO2 not improving despite third IM epi
A third IM epi has been given. Despite maximal medical therapy, swelling continues to progress. The crew now faces a critical decision: is the airway still passable with an endotracheal tube via video laryngoscopy, or has it progressed to the point where the ETT will not pass and a surgical airway is the primary plan? The crew must commit to a double-setup: simultaneous preparation for both attempts.
