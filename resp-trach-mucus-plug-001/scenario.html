Expected Actions
CRITICAL Disconnect the home ventilator — eliminate equipment as a cause
CRITICAL Remove inner cannula if present — can plug independently of outer
CRITICAL Provide passive oxygenation — high-flow O2 over nose/mouth AND over the stoma to avoid desaturation during troubleshooting
CRITICAL Instill 1-3 mL sterile saline into the trach and attempt to pass an 8 French suction catheter (size = 2x trach size = 8 Fr for a 4.0 trach)
CRITICAL If suction catheter will NOT pass, recognize complete obstruction and proceed directly to emergent tracheostomy change
CRITICAL Prepare a NEW 4.0 cuffed trach (same size) from the family's go-bag; lubricate the obturator; have a 3.5 trach and a 3.5 mm ETT ready as backup
CRITICAL Apply cardiac monitor, SpO2, waveform capnography (will confirm successful ventilation after replacement)
