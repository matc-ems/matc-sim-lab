Arrival — Hypertensive Flash Pulmonary Edema
Classic hypertensive flash pulmonary edema. This is a problem of volume DISTRIBUTION (afterload) more than total body fluid overload. Treatment centerpiece: CPAP for positive pressure ventilation to redistribute fluid, and aggressive nitroglycerin for afterload reduction. Do NOT rush to intubate — medical therapy works quickly.

CRITICAL Apply oxygen while preparing CPAP
CRITICAL Apply CPAP starting at 5 cmH2O, titrate up to 10 cmH2O as tolerated; this is the single most important intervention

IMPORTANT Do NOT administer furosemide — it provides no immediate benefit and the problem is distribution, not total body water
